Ingredients

For the Patty

  • Ground beef (80/20 for juiciness)
  • Salt
  • Black peper
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Onion powder (optional)

For the bun

  • Burger buns
  • Butter (toasting)

For Toppings

  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and optional seasonings. Shape into patties.
  2. Butter and toast until golden brown.
  3. Grill or pan-fry for 3-4 min per side. Add cheese in the last minute.
  4. Spread sauce on the bun, add lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and the patty.
  5. Top with the bun, secure if needed, and dig in!
