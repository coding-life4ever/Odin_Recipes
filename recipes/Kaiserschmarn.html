Kaiserschmarrn



Description

A fluffy, caramelized Austrian pancake, shredded into bite-sized pieces, lightly crispy on the edges, and served with powdered sugar and fruit compote. A sweet delight fit for an emperor!


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, and salt into a smooth batter.
  2. Melt butter in a pan, pour in the batter, and cook until golden.
  3. Sprinkle raisins, flip, and cook briefly.
  4. Tear into pieces, add more butter, and caramelize.
  5. Serve with powdered sugar and fruit compote.
