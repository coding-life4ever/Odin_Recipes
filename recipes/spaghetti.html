Spaghetti

Description

Twirl into a plate of perfection—our rich, savory spaghetti dish features a luscious, slow-simmered sauce coating perfectly cooked pasta, delivering a comforting and satisfying bite every time. A true classic made with love!


Ingredients

  • Spagehtti Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Boil salted water, cook spaghetti, drain.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add meat (optional), cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, season, and simmer.
  5. Mix spaghetti with sauce, serve with cheese.
  6. Enjoy! 🍝😋
